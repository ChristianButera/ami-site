About Us

Our Objective is Simple

Deliver Quality Product, On Time and at a Fair, Competitive Price

AMI is your source for custom electrical control panels, electrical enclosures, electrical mechanical assemblies, and cable assemblies. AMI specializes in design, manufacture, and assembly of integrated mechanical and electrical products. With our broad capabilities we can offer the customer a total turn-key solution: from engineering and design; to electrical and mechanical assembly; to total panel and enclosure assembly.

AMI provides engineering, design, manufacturing and assembly services for various industries
